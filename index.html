WHY REDLINE

Your markup belongs in the document — not in someone else's app.

01

Markup that travels

Comments, highlights, strikeouts, ink, and shapes are saved as standard PDF annotations — not trapped in a proprietary app. Send the file to anyone and your review opens intact in any PDF reader. No account, no plugin, no lock-in.

02

Nothing to install, nothing stored

RedLine runs in your browser. Your documents are processed only to apply the edits you make and are never stored on our servers. Review states — status, assignee, notes — stay on your device.

03

Your Drive, only the files you pick

Connect a Google account to open and save PDFs from Drive. RedLine uses per-file access only — it can see the files you choose, never the rest of your Drive — and never uses your documents to train AI.
